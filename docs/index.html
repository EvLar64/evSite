Francis Ford Coppola's dystopian epic, Megalopolis is coming out later in September. The official trailer can be found here.

With a production spanning multiple decades, the movie will most likely serve as Coppola's final artistic statement and contribution to the landscape of cinema.

After all, the man sold off a huge chunk of his own wine empire in order to fund the production of the movie.

What other films has Coppola been reponsible for?

All of the following films (plus several more):

  1. The Godfather (I - III)
  2. The Conversation
  3. Apocalypse Now
  4. The Outsiders
  5. Bram Stoker's Dracula

Who can we look forward to seeing in Megalopolis?

The cast is absolutely stacked, including (but not limited to) the following big names:

  • Adam Driver
  • Giancarlo Esposito
  • Shia LaBeouf
  • Aubrey Plaza
  • Lawrence Fishburne
  • Dustin Hoffman
