  • Who is Francis Ford Coppola?

Francis Ford Coppola's dystopian epic, Megalopolis, is coming out on September 27, 2024. The official trailer can be found here.

With a production spanning multiple decades, the movie will most likely serve as Coppola's final artistic statement and contribution to the landscape of cinema.

After all, the man sold off a huge chunk of his own wine empire in order to fund its production.

What other films has Coppola been reponsible for?

All (but not limited to) the following films:

  1. The Godfather [series] (1972, 1974, 1990)
  2. The Conversation (1974)
  3. Apocalypse Now (1979)
  4. The Outsiders (1983)
  5. Bram Stoker's Dracula (1992)

Who can we look forward to seeing in Megalopolis?

The cast is absolutely stacked, including the following big names, and more:

  • Adam Driver
  • Giancarlo Esposito
  • Shia LaBeouf
  • Aubrey Plaza
  • Laurence Fishburne
