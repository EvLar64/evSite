PROS OF VIRTUAL PRODUCTION ^

  • More environmentally sustainable than other forms of production
  • Consistently-advancing technology/realism
  • LED Volume: Gives an actor more to respond to than green/blue screen or performance capture (in most cases)
  • LED Volume: More organic, situational scene lighting
  • Different disciplines are becoming much more intertwined (Film/VFX/Game Design - good or bad?)
  • More AI implementation (good or bad?)
  • Other positive applications outside of film
  • Data/sources (visual of how LED Volumes work?)

CONS OF VIRTUAL PRODUCTION ^

  • More reliance on Unreal Engine (and other game technologies) is required, and has created a gap between VFX education and current VFX knowledge requirements
  • Sky/floor camera movement limitations, perception disadvantages, space limitations
  • Rendering/capturing/dressing big LED displays requires a lot of time, money, resources, and testing
  • A lot of Virtual Production work nowadays means that post-production comes first, in terms of finalizing the look of a rendered scene before shooting rather than after (earlier digital practices)
